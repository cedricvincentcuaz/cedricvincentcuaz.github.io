Welcome to my website ! I am a Postdoctoral Researcher at the Signal Processing Laboratory LTS4 of the Swiss Federal Institute of Technology EPFL, under the supervision of Pr. Pascal Frossard. I am working on Optimal Transport, Graph Representation Learning and its applications in Digital Pathology in the context of the PHRT project . I completed my PhD on Optimal Transport for Graph Representation Learning in March 2023, under the supervision of Pr.Rémi Flamary at Pr. Marco Corneli. Past then I was part of the 3IA Côte d'Azur and MAASAI team within INRIA Sophia-Antipolis Méditerranée. Prior to my PhD, I graduated from ENSAE Paris - IP Paris in applied mathematics and machine learning.
